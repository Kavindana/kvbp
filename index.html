National Identity & Authenticity

My Sri Lankan Roots

Proudly Sri Lankan, Creatively Global

My sense of self is deeply bound with my roots. I carry the symbols of Sri Lankan identity in small, everyday ways - whether it's wearing the green-and-gold jersey of the Sri Lanka cricket team or having a Lion flag pin on my laptop.

In my creative projects, I often weave in local context: a web page might feature Sri Lankan art, a game might use music inspired by traditional drums, a story might echo a Sinhalese folktale. I want my creations to speak truth about my world, not just mimic global trends.

"Does it mean feeling your heart swell at the Lion flag? ... supporting the Sri Lankan cricket team?" For me, the answer is a wholehearted yes.
